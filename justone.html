Just One

Draw the round, then tap a spotlight when it's that round's turn to reveal the mystery word.

Tap draw to line up 7 mystery words for tonight's rounds, in order.

How to Play

3–7 players · ~20 minutes

  • Each round, one player is the guesser and steps back or closes their eyes.
  • Everyone else secretly writes one single-word clue for the mystery word.
  • Clues are compared. Any clue that matches another player's clue is cancelled and hidden.
  • Other rules about clues
    • Your clue must be just one word.
    • Numbers, acronyms, onomatopoeia and special characters are allowed (007, $, SMS, Riiiiing).
    • It cannot be the word spelt differently (shurt / shirt).
    • Or in a foreign language (Poisson / Fish).
    • It cannot be from the same family (prince / princess).
    • a phonetically identical word (Whether / Weather)
      • Or an invented word (Swee’ting / Cake).>
  • The guesser sees only the surviving unique clues and gets one guess.
  • Correct guess = a point for the whole table. It's fully cooperative — everyone wins or loses together.
  • Rotate the guesser each round and play through all 7 (or 13) words to get a final score.
