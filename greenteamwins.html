GREEN TEAM WINS

Tap Generate, then flip each card to reveal the round's question.

Hit generate to deal 15 cards — 5 Fill in the Blank, 5 This or That, and 5 Best of Three.

How to Play

3–12 players · ~15 minutes

  • Everyone starts on the Orange Team.
  • Reveal one question. Everyone secretly writes their own answer at the same time.
  • Don't write the smartest answer — write what you think most people at the table will write.
  • Reveal all answers together. Whoever matches the majority joins (or stays on) the Green Team.
  • Scoring: staying Green→Green = 2 points, moving Orange→Green = 1 point, anyone left on Orange = 0.
  • If there's a tie for most popular answer, the whole table joins Green and scores 2. If everyone writes something different, everyone goes Orange.
  • Play all 15 cards — highest score wins.
